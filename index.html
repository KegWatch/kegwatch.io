--- title: KegWatch description: Smart, fun, and simple keg monitoring at home! ---

The fun beer monitoring solution

Track keg levels, beer consumption, and compete with friends in real time!



Every pour tracked in detail

Every pour is automatically tracked, no matter which tap or who pours it. If you want to pour a beer without tagging someone, no problem! The beer will automatically log as Anonymous.

By attaching a barcode or QR code scanner to KegWatch you are able to scan yourself in before opening the tap and have the pour tagged to you!

View beer popularity over time

KegWatch knows when kegs are tapped and when they are kicked. Once the "remaining beer" hits 0oz, KegWatch automatically logs the date and the number of days it took to kick the keg.

Not sure what your next keg should be? Just look back and see which one was the most popular!

Advanced data visualization to really understand your beer stats

Tables of data are informative, but they can also be boring. Our platform allows you to visualize your beer data in different ways to keep the experience informative, fun, and competitve!

Simple, unobtrusive installation

Ease of installation sets KegWatch apart from other keg management solutions. No severed beer lines. No scales. No electronics inside the kegerator. Simply place the unit on top of your beer tower, attach the included magnets to your tap handles, and you are ready to go!

KegWatch looks for the tap handles to open and close and estimates the amount of beer poured based on the flow rate of your draft system!
